About

This project analyzes Siemens’ cash flow performance across fiscal years using publicly available financial reports.

Data Sources

Annual reports published by Siemens AG (IFRS). Figures are extracted directly from consolidated cash flow statements.

Calculations

Free Cash Flow = Operating Cash Flow − Capital Expenditures Free Cash Flow Conversion is calculated relative to Operating Cash Flow.

Limitations

Data is extracted from PDFs and may contain minor inconsistencies. This project is for educational and analytical purposes only.
